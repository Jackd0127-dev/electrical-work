# Electrical Safety & Services in Northern Ireland

Static customer-facing electrical safety and services site for `electrical.scriptai.space`.

## Files

- `index.html` - the deployed static page.
- `sitemap.xml` - search engine sitemap for the static page sections.
- `robots.txt` - crawler policy pointing at the sitemap.

## Publication Notes

This site was rewritten to remove unsafe DIY electrical installation instructions, terminal-level diagrams, cable sizing recipes, consumer unit wiring guidance, procedural testing guidance, and over-confident Northern Ireland regulatory claims.

The site is for general customer information only. It is not a DIY wiring guide.

## Manual Review Required Before Publication

A qualified electrician or appropriate compliance reviewer must manually confirm:

- bathroom zone wording or any future bathroom diagrams
- consumer unit wording
- EV charger wording and NIE Networks process wording
- garage, outbuilding and earthing arrangement wording
- earthing and protective bonding wording
- smoke, heat and carbon monoxide alarm wording and any future layout diagrams
- EICR and private tenancy electrical safety wording
- BS 7671 edition and amendment references
- Building Control references
- any future diagrams showing conductors, circuits or protective devices
- all service claims, service areas, qualifications, insurance, accreditations, reviews and contact details

Do not remove these review requirements without evidence of qualified review.
